# Points

The Tizi points system is designed to reward long-term protocol participants. Points are directly tied to the user's asset holdings in the protocol and the duration of those holdings. Points are accumulated daily: 4 points are earned for every 1 TD equivalent held for 1 full day (1 TD / 1 DAY / 4 points).1 point is earned for every 1 stTD equivalent held for 1 full day (1 stTD / 1 DAY / 1 point). Holdings for less than 6 hours do not count. Points are calculated based on the amount of TD and stTD held in the user's wallet, with cross-chain holdings aggregated. Points can be used to participate in exclusive Tizi ecosystem events, lottery games, and future governance benefits.
